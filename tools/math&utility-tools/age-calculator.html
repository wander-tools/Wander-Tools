How to Use the Age Calculator

  • Select your birth date using the date picker
  • Optionally select a different date to compare to (default is today)
  • Click the "Calculate Age" button to see your age
  • Use the "Reset" button to clear all inputs and results
  • View your age in years, months, weeks, and days
  • See additional information like total months, weeks, and days
  • Find out how many days until your next birthday
  • Discover your zodiac sign based on your birth date
  • Toggle between dark and light mode using the moon/sun button
