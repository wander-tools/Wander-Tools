Scanner

Scan QR codes and barcodes from your camera or uploaded images

How to Use

  • Click "Start Camera" to scan QR codes using your device camera
  • Or upload an image file containing a QR code
  • Position the QR code within the scanning frame
  • Results will appear automatically below
  • If the result is a URL, you can open it directly
  • WiFi QR Codes: If you scan a WiFi QR code, the network name and password will be displayed. Click "Connect to WiFi" for connection instructions.
